How to Sedler!

You have six tries to guess the word

The word has to be a valid five letter word. Hit enter to submit your guess

HINT: The word has to do with the Pesach Sedar.

With each incorrect guess, Sedler will point out letters that is in the word, and where they are placed correctly.

Examples

The letter W is in the word and in the correct spot.

S W O R D

The letter A is in the word but is placed in the wrong spot.

H U M A N

The letter D is not in the word.

E C H A D
